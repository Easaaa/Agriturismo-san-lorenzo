Description

We offer open-space apartments with 3 rooms: living room/kitchen (fully furnished), bathroom and bedroom.

Each accommodation disposes of a double bed, a sofa bed in the living room and/or in the bedroom and a private bathroom with a shower.

The living room is equipped with a dining table, some chairs and a sofa. If needed, you can also enjoy the wood stove heating.

Important information

Check-in / Check-out

The check-in is allowed from 2:00 pm to 6:00 pm. The check-out limit is 11:00 am.

The route IMPORTANT

We suggest you set your navigator to “Gaino”. There’s a possibility that it will lead you to Navazzo, DO NOT follow this direction. Once you get to Toscolano Maderno, go to Gaino and enter in the Camerate valley and proceed 5 km. You will drive on a dirt unpaved road, so we suggest you proceed carefully while enjoying the view. Any effort will be paid back once you get to our quiet holiday farm accommodation.
